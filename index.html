Welcome to The Slackers' Chords. This website is pretty much about making The Slackers' music even more apreciable -- if that's possible -- by allowing the fans of the band to play the songs themselves.

In the 'The Band' section, you'll find a short biography of the band, as well as a discography, listing each album and its year of release.
